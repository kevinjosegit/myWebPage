Nutella and Penut Butter Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Nutella
  • Peanut
  • Half banana
  • Butter, softened
  • Sandwich bread

Method:

  1. Heat a skillet to medium heat
  2. Spread one side of the bread with peanut butter, and the other side with Nutella
  3. Add slices of a banana and close the sandwich
  4. Butter one side of the bread and place it on the skillet
  5. Spread a layer of butter on the other side while the sandwich is cooking
  6. Cook until lightly golden and flip to the other side
  7. cook again until golden and remove from heat
  8. let it cool for a moment and then enjoy
